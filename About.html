Get to know Crosshair Media and South Africa's student youth market like never before.

Who We Are

Ask yourself this question, how much do you really know about South Africa’s student youth market?

At Crosshair Media we’ve made it our mission to get to know these young game changers, and we would be more than happy to make an introduction.

As a leader in the influencer marketing category, we have built our reputation on creating relatable connections between brands and students. We have positioned ourselves to offer you unprecedented access to this youth market segment through our network of student accommodation providers. Together we can bring about relevant and authentic engagement, and build your brand by crafting meaningful stories.

Our Story

We started as a niche business in Johannesburg, South Africa, and soon realized that we have a rare relationship with this unique, diverse, and active community of empowered individuals. We put a lot of effort into nurturing this relationship, expanding our reach throughout Mzansi, and evolving with this market to ensure we don’t lose sight of their specific needs.

This relationship gives us one-of-a-kind access that allows us to carry out surveys and set up focus groups, and to generate analytics that provide the data and insights that you and your brand need to ensure you work together optimally.

Let’s Build Something Meaningful

Speak to us if you’d like to originate relevant, highly targeted offers that drive awareness, consideration, acquisition, and ultimately brand advocacy. As an invested partner, we’ll be there to spark innovation and explore better product experiences with our student base.
